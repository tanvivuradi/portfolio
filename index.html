Projects

Designing Courses for BC Pharmacists

Led the design of pharmacist training courses supporting BC’s Ministry of Health initiative to expand prescribing authority. The program reached 2,500+ learners, with over 75% reporting positive outcomes, equipping pharmacists to confidently manage minor ailments and lab test orders

UX Research for Children's Educational Social Robot

This research explored how a social robot, Haru, can deliver negative feedback to children (ages 5–12) to improve classroom engagement. Results showed 60% of children preferred Haru as a study companion, highlighting the importance of emotionally supportive feedback in human-AI interactions.

3D Modelling with Blender GIS

Supported the development of a 3D campus model for a graduate course on immersive technologies at UBCO under Professor Pourang Irani. The model served as the base for an AR/VR project, allowing students to design and interact with the environment.
